Necessary Changes in Society

— Richard Stallman

Fundamental

  • replace plutocracy and tyranny with democracy
  • defend freedom of expression
  • increase social bonding, so money is not the ultimate answer

Survival of Civilization

  • stop global heating and ocean acidification
  • end pollution: toxic, plastic, and antibiotic
  • institute sustainable agriculture
  • establish abortion and contraception rights
  • avoid future megadeaths by avoiding many births
  • protect wildlife and ecosystems
  • deconcentrate industry
  • create speed bumps to slow recruiting fanatics online

Well-being for everyone

  • provide gratis medical care for all
  • end prejudice (racism, sexism, antisemitism, caste prejudice, and others)
  • lift everyone out of poverty
  • end nonfree software
  • end online dis-services
  • establish freedom to share all published works

Education for everyone

  • provide good, humane, non-exploitative education for all
  • provide education in critical thinking
  • provide education in conflict resolution
  • provide practical sex/love education:
    respect, saying yes, saying no, hearing yes, hearing no, recognizing joy, making joy
  • provide non-tendentious history education
  • reinvigorate and strengthen journalism
